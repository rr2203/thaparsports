Events

TIET organizes various sports events such as:

Inter Year Competition,Inter hostel Games,Thaparlympics(Inter department competitions),Annual Sports Meet and Inter Technology University Tournaments. To promote and encourage sports amongst the Engineering students we also organize a unique national level sports fest named 'URJA'.

URJA include team and athletic events. For this sports fest we invite IITs, NITs and other premier Engineering institutes from all over India.
